What is Pair Programming

A shared work space between two individuals to create programs. Consists of a driver and a navigator. Can be done in person or remotely and is popular within several companies.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

It helps with generating ideas and aids in catching bugs in code. Helps bridge gaps in understanding so that people learn more quickly.

Compare Driver and Navigator

The driver is manipulating the code while the navigator is feeding in information.

Reflection

Pairing with my partner worked out great. We worked together well and each took turns driving and navigating. We both contributed ideas and helped eachother out. I look forward to pair programming more. I felt like our technical skill levels were a bit different but we helped eachother and it went great.
